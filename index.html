About Me

Shaping Futures Through Education

I am Vivian Atieno Odhiambo, a dedicated and passionate lecturer at Kenia College, where I teach both Information & Communication Technology (ICT) and Certified Public Accountancy (CPA). My mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application.

With a strong academic foundation and industry recognition from leading platforms, I bring both technical expertise and professional depth to every classroom. I believe education is the most powerful tool for transformation.

My journey is defined by continuous learning — from earning industry certifications to receiving awards from platforms like Ajira Digital and BrighterMonday.
